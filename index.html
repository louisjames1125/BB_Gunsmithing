Refinishing

We offer gun refinishing services for rifles, handguns and shotguns, stocks repaired cracks fixed and even wood patched. Some hot blueing is done with an outside source. All cold blueing is done in house. Our coatings can be used to coat a wide range of properly prepared substrates for all metal types including titanium, aluminum and magnesium.. All plastics and polymers used in firearms frames, stocks are repaired by outside sources.

Optics
